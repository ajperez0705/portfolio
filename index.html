Client Work

The following projects were websites made for local businsses using Adobe XD, Wordpress and the Elementor plugin. During this time, I did not touch a single line of code (aside from custom CSS at times). The intention here is to show that I have designed and delivered actual products to actual people with actual deadlines.

Martin's BBQ

Martin's BBQ was a local Latin American restaurant who needed a brand new website for their new opening. The goal was to provide newcomers with information about the cuisine, as well as their menu. Customers can also order from GrubHub.

Live Site

Felipe Callado Photography

Felipe Callado was a photographer who would regularly use Facebook to book new clients. He grew to a point where a proper website that showcased his talents was needed. A very minimalistic design, something that is different than I was used to, but one that the client and I enjoyed in the end.

Live Site

High Soccer Arena

High Soccer Arena is an indoor soccer facility. There were a number of goals for this site: provide tournament information for walk-in visitors, information and registration links for parents and their children, and the ability to reserve fields and birthday parties. Additionally, I was tasked with improving the brands SEO rankings through keyword research and site architecture.
